The protocol transcript (which allows you to verify the parameters,
and compare with the attestations) is also available over BitTorrent:

https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/powersoftau-transcript/powersoftau-transcript.torrent

SHA256:
transcript:
605609b98e339554832394181a3aa5880c48cffb0b603ca0dd7ce47760ca2cbe

It has also been uploaded to the Internet Archive:

https://archive.org/details/transcript_201804

The transcript can be verified using a tool we've constructed:

https://github.com/ebfull/powersoftau/blob/master/src/bin/verify.rs

As before, we're hosting the attestations of participants here:

https://github.com/ZcashFoundation/powersoftau-attestations

We plan to make an announcement soon on the Zcash Foundation blog. I'd
like to thank everyone who helped make this possible. All of the
participants did a wonderful job, and I'm very happy with the diversity
and openness of the ceremony. Many members of the crypto and
cryptocurrency communities participated or played witness to what took
place. I'd like to thank Jason Davies for doing a wonderful job as
a coordinator for the final months.

I would also like to thank my co-authors Ariel Gabizon and Ian Miers
for their invaluable work on the paper. And last but not least, a huge
thanks to the Zcash Foundation!

Sean Bowe
